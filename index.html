About Me

I'm Neo Cruz, a passionate music producer, graphic designer, and creative director. My journey in the music industry has allowed me to work with renowned artists, and my production for Denise Julia's track "Butterflies" has garnered over 23 million streams on Spotify. My love for music transcends genres, and I specialize in creating unique, high-quality tracks that resonate with listeners.

As a graphic designer, I bring a creative and artistic vision to all my projects. My work ranges from designing track covers to creating visually striking graphics, posters, and other media. I believe that the visual representation of music is just as important as the sound, and I strive to create designs that capture the essence of the music they accompany. My designs for Hulyan's "Sensations" and Clouded's "Bakamagalitbfmo" are just a few examples of my work.

In addition to my work in music and graphic design, I am also the creative director for Skandalouz, an organization that hosts parties and events. As their lead graphic designer, I am responsible for developing the visual identity and promotional materials for each event.

Whether it's music production, graphic design, or creative direction, I am committed to delivering excellence and bringing creative visions to life. Let's create something amazing together!

My Services
